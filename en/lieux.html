Places to Visit

Discoveries in Nice

Old Nice

Colorful alleys, Italian-style facades, lively squares, markets, and local specialties: Old Nice is the historic heart of the city. Stroll through its typical streets to discover artisan shops, baroque churches, and sunny cafés.

Google Maps

Castle Hill

Offering one of the best views of the Bay of Angels, Castle Hill is a green haven. Accessible on foot or by elevator, it features ruins, waterfalls, playgrounds, and stunning views of the port and the old town.

Google Maps

Promenade des Anglais

The famous Promenade des Anglais runs along the sea for several kilometers. Ideal for walking, biking, or simply watching the sunset, it offers a unique atmosphere of palm trees, iconic hotels, and pebble beaches.

Google Maps

Try a Socca

You can't visit Nice without tasting socca, a chickpea flatbread cooked over a wood fire. Enjoy it hot with a sprinkle of pepper in a typical Old Nice café or at the Libération market.

Google Maps

Place Masséna

With its red buildings, arcades, fountains, and checkered floor, Place Masséna is the vibrant center of Nice. Surrounded by gardens and close to all attractions, it’s a must-see between the sea and the old town.
